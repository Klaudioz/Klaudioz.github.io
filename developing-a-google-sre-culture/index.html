What is SRE? üßê

  • It’s both a practice and a job role, where engineering directly supports software operations.
  • At Google, SRE is described by its founder as “what happens when you ask a software engineer to design an operations team.”
  • SRE approach is a concrete way to solve problems that the DevOps philosophy addresses.
  • Mission: To protect, provide for, and progress software and systems with consistent focus on availability, latency, performance, and capacity.

What is the problem? üî•

  • Customers aren’t happy but everything is “green”. There is something wrong.
  • Developers and operations teams working in silos with different priorities.

  • Accept failure as normal.
  • Reduce organizational silos.
  • Implement gradual change.
  • Leverage tooling and automation.
  • Measure everything.

  • It’s done by documenting and conducting a blameless Postmortem.
